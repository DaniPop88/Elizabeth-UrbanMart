About Us

Our Story
Founded in 2017, Elizabeth Sagun Urban Mart was born from a simple dream — to make stylish, quality furniture accessible to everyday Filipino families. Starting as a humble neighborhood shop in Barangay Poblacion, we've grown into a trusted source of affordable home solutions for residents of Muntinlupa and nearby areas.

  • Maaasahan – We stand by the quality and durability of our products.
  • Malapit sa Puso – We curate items that reflect Filipino taste, tradition, and practicality.
  • Makabago at Abot-Kaya – We blend modern trends with affordable pricing.

Our Mission: To help Filipino households build beautiful and comfortable living spaces by offering affordable yet premium homeware and furniture.

Our Vision: To be the go-to urban mart for families seeking reliable, aesthetic, and budget-friendly home solutions in Southern Metro Manila.
